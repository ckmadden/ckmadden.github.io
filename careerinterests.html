Career interests

Astrophysics is a branch of space science that applies the laws of physics
and chemistry to explain the birth, life and death of stars, planets,
galaxies, nebulae and other objects in the universe. It has two sibling
sciences, astronomy and cosmology, and the lines between them blur.

This is University of California, Berkeley and it is one of the best colleges
for astrophysics.

Requirements
Salary $49k-120k per year
Skills
  • critical-thinking
  • research skills
  • attention to detail
  • extensive math and physics knowledge
Experience experience in...
  • astronomy
  • physics
  • engineering
Education six years just for a bachelor's degree and
five to seven more years for graduate school.

Architects create designs for new construction projects, alterations and
redevelopments. They use their specialist construction knowledge and
high-level drawing skills to design buildings that are functional, safe,
sustainable and aesthetically pleasing.

This is Cornell University and it is one of the best colleges for Architecture.

Requirements
Salary $51k-91k per year
Skills
  • design skills and knowledge.
  • knowledge of building and construction.
  • to be thorough and pay attention to detail.
Experience experience in...
  • Architectural Design History and Theory.
  • Building design
  • Mathematics
  • Physical Sciences.
Education Most architects earn their degree through
a 5-year Bachelor of Architecture degree program.
Many earn a master's degree in architecture,
which can take 1 to 5 additional years.
